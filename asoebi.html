What is Aso-Ebi?

Unity in Fabric

Aso-Ebi — meaning "family cloth" in Yoruba — is the tradition of wearing coordinated outfits at celebrations. It is a visual declaration of love, solidarity, and belonging.

At Eleven08, we bring the same precision and luxury to group Aso-Ebi orders as we do to every individual piece. From the fabric selection to the finishing, every outfit in your group will reflect the standard you set.
