About Eleven08

Our Story

Eleven08 was created for that individual with a sense of deep heritage and style, who understands presence and isn't afraid to break new grounds.

We believe clothing is more than fabric. It is memory, identity, and intention. Every piece from Eleven08 is designed with that conviction at its core — a deliberate marriage of African textile heritage and contemporary silhouette.

Our work spans ready-to-wear and fully bespoke custom commissions. Whether you are stepping into a room for the first time or returning to one you own, we want you dressed exactly as you feel inside: formidable.

We are based in Lagos, Nigeria, and serve clients nationwide and across the diaspora. Every piece is made with precision, care, and a deep respect for the traditions that inspire us.
